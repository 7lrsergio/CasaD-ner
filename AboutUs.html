About Casa Doner

Authentic flavors, warm hospitality

Our Story

Casa Doner was born from a passion for authentic Mediterranean cuisine and the desire to bring the rich flavors of traditional doner to our community.

Founded in 2015, we started as a small family kitchen with a single dream: to serve the most delicious, freshly-prepared doner using recipes passed down through generations.

Every dish we serve tells a story of heritage, quality ingredients, and the love we put into every meal.

A small Mediterranean shop offering olives, cheeses, pastries, and specialty products imported from their home region.
